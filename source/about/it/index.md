## Public

We encourage non-members to subscribe to our [public mailing list](https://lists.isdcf.com/g/isdcf/topics) and [public calendar](https://lists.isdcf.com/g/isdcf/calendar) to remain informed of ISDCF activities.

## Member-only

Our [member site](https://lists.isdcf.com/g/members/) allows members to subscribe to our [member-only calendar](https://lists.isdcf.com/g/members/calendar), consult [mailing list archives](https://lists.isdcf.com/g/members/topics) and access [member-only files](https://lists.isdcf.com/g/members/files).
