## Inter-Society Digital Cinema Forum (ISDCF)

### Mission

ISDCF was formed in May 2006 to promote interactive dialogue, information exchange, and practical solutions for the manufacture, distribution, and exhibition of content in the Digital Cinema ecosystem.

### Membership

We have {% url_for 'members' '/about/membership/' %} from across the cinema ecosystem, including Studios, Exhibitors, Integrators, Equipment Manufacturers, Consultants, and other committees.

{% url_for 'Become a member today!' '/about/membership/' %}

### Participation

We hold meetings about every 6 weeks either hybrid (remote and in-person) or virtual (remote-only). We encourage remote participation. Meeting notes are made {% url_for 'available publicly' '/events/notes/' %}.

Our meetings are open to members and guests in good standing. They are subject to our {% url_for 'Terms of Participation' '/about/terms/' %}.

Our [member site](https://lists.isdcf.com/g/members/) allows members to subscribe to our member calendar, consult mailing list archives and access member-only files.

We encourage non-members to subscribe to our [public mailing list](https://lists.isdcf.com/g/isdcf/topics) and [public calendar](https://lists.isdcf.com/g/isdcf/calendar) to be informed of public events.
